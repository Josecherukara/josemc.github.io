About Me

I am a theoretical physicist working at the Department of Physics, The Cochin College, Cochin. My research focuses on extending general relativity to strong gravity regimes. The universe happens to be at extremely high energies shortly after Big-bang, an area of physics still at Dark. Also, I study Black holes under different theories of gravity. We believe Einstein's GR doesn't hold at close vicinities of Black holes.
